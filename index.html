The graduation class of 2019 were inducted to the school of pharmacy on January 2014. They were registered in the Chiromo Campus and asssigned hostels in Mamlaka.

Journey Till Now

The years of study are divided into five.
The first two are preclinical years while the last three are clinically oriented. The following is the break down of some of the key subjects undertaken in the first two years of study.

  1. Year One: Anatomy, Physiology and Basic Maths
    2. Anatomy
    The study of the structure of the body
    Physiology
    The study of the function of the body
    Basic Maths
    Learn useless things like sets and calculus, if you fancy maths without numbers, you are in for a treat.
  2. Year Two: Biochemistry, Pharmacology, and Microbilogy
    3. Biochemistry
    Study of the body's chemical reactions,
    Microbiology
    A pain in the tush study of the bugs, and not the computer kind.
    Pharmacy School courses
    Start of the real study of the course

The first two years of study are split in between the chiromo campus and the Kenyatta Hospital Campus. The whole of the first year is undertaken in chiromo campus while the second year was evenly split between the two locations.

  • Chiromo - Biochemistry, Anatomy, Physiology, Maths, Chemistry(inorganic and organic)
  • Kenyatta - Pharmacy based courses and microbiology.
