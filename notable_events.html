Notable Events During The Duration of Study

Home

A number of events had a long lasting impact on the duration and quality of our education. This included:

  • Two student strikes triggered by election wrangles in the election of SONU officials.

    • This necessitted a change in the governance structure of the student body.

  • The formation of a new student body representaion body with different election rules. The new body is called UNSA
  • Major lecturers strike, two of them actually, that took more than three months each. One was in August and the other was in January
  • Two general elections were held within months apart.

    • The consequences of these were that the years of study were extended by one more year, hence the 2019 graduation.
