Pharmacy School Courses

The pharmacy school situated within the college of health sciences campus in Kenyatta National Hospital was the main place of study for the majority of our studies starting from the second year. It is in second year that we were introduced to classes that directly tie into the courses. These included:

  • Pharmacology
    •
  • Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Clinical Pharmacy
The second year was split evenly between Chiromo and Kenyatta.
